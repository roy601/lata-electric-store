Payment Guide

Cash on Delivery: Order is placed online, customer pays on delivery. Best for first-time customers.

bKash / Nagad: Customer sends payment before dispatch. Add your merchant number and provide clear payment instructions.

Tip: Always verify bKash/Nagad payments before shipping the order.
